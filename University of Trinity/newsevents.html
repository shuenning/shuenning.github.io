News & Events

University of Trinity in partnership with Code First Girls

University of Trinity is hosting a number of events and activities during the first week of March as part of International Women’s Day.

Film, Television and Media students visit XPLOR Centre for Virtual Production

Film, Television and Media students from University of Trinity recently had the opportunity to attend a training day at the XPLOR Centre for Virtual Production: one of the largest production and research centres of its kind in Northern England.

Mosaic artwork brings strories to life

The ‘Little Bits of God’ artwork is currently being hosted in the Chapel at University of Trinity and helps bring scriptures and key gospels to life in a modern and engaging way.

Bank of England event held at University of Trinity

A Bank of England Citizens’ Panel has been held in Trinity to explore the cost of living crisis with students.

University of Trinity alumnus and Professor receive recognition Leads

University of Trinity alumnus Fr Marc Pitson has been ordained to the priesthood, and Revd Professor Jane de Gay has been appointed Associate Priest and Lecturer at Leeds Minster.

Writer's Festival providing opportunities to work with professional storytellers

The University of Trinity Writers’ Festival is an annual event made up of readings, a book stall and masterclass workshops in creative writing.
