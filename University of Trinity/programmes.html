Programmes

  • Foundation in Business

The programme provides a flexible structured study programmed to enhance student knowledge and skills in various aspects including improvement in English language and communication skills.

Students will gain a knowledge base in basic business studies and introduce fundamentals business concepts. This would encourage the development of a creative attitude, an open mind and business-relevant awareness. Participants will understand how organisations are managed effectively and managed effectively and how to launch and sustain their managerial careers.

Upon successful completion of this programme, students are eligible to enrol into our degree programme, creating a seamless transition towards pursuing a future in business.

  • Diploma in Business Management

The Diploma in Business Management at University of Trinity is designed to provide the skills and knowledge for students to work efficiently and effectively in their workplace by embedding in-depth theoretical and practical knowledge. Graduates of this Business Management programme will demonstrate integrative knowledge and comprehension of key concepts, principles and practices in the field of business management.

Whether students are looking to be self-employed and establish their own business, work for international corporations, start-ups, or non-profits, or even work for government agencies, this Business Management programme will provide them with skills and knowledge to enhance their careers. Students will be taught to have a solid understanding of modern and up-to-date approaches to business by integrating academic theory, business management knowledge, and skills development in a range of fields.

Students will be exposed to management practice, organizational theory, and the international business environment, as well as other aspects of business that would prepare them to become successful business administrators. Students may also be motivated to develop innovative and analytical thinking skills, leaving them with strong business acumen. The Diploma in Business Management at University of Trinity will provide graduates with the competitive edge they need in excel their career paths of choice.

  • Bachelor of Science (Honours) in Business Management

By taking a degree in Business Management, you will develop a broad understanding of business organisations. You will learn how businesses develop strategies, make decisions, organise their internal resources, and manage risk and uncertainty.

Business and marketing case studies form an integral part of many of the modules, especially during the later stages of your course programme. In a case study, information is presented about a firm’s products, markets, financial structure, human resources and other factors that may influence its success. During your course, you will discuss and analyse strategies and actions that the firm should take in the future.

Your degree in Business Management will provide insights and expertise in specific business functions such as marketing, human resource management, accounting and finance. You will study specialised topics such as entrepreneurship and international business. You will also develop an awareness of the wider environment in which businesses operate, by studying subjects such as business economics and business law.
