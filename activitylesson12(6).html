Recipe of Chicken Curry


Ingredients:
  • 800g chicken
  • 1/2-1 cocomut milk
  • 180g curry paste
  • 3 large potato
  • 3 large onion
  • 3 stalks curry leaves

Steps:
  1. Saute curry paste in hot oil until fragent
  2. Add in chicken
  3. Continue to saute until chicken releases oil
  4. Add in water, temarind paste, curry leaves, potato, onion, sugar, salt
  5. Let chicken simmer in slow fire until potatoes becomes soft and chicken is cooked throughly
  6. Add in coconut milk and cook until oil starts separate
  7. Remove and serve
